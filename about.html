Who Am I?

Here is an astronomy-lover. Hello, My name is Nur Sima Karhan. I absolutely love talking about physics, astronomy, and astrophysics. But it's not just about talking—I want to truly understand, explore, and even contribute to science with my own projects.

I'm also really into coding. I see programming not just as a skill, but as a powerful tool to unlock the secrets of the universe. That's why I'm learning C, C++, Python, HTML, and CSS. From developing an AI model that classifies galaxies to building robots, I love turning ideas into reality.

I believe science isn't something you just read about—it's something you experience, question, and bring to life. That's why I enjoy sharing what I learn through TEDx-style presentations, where I break down complex topics into engaging and thought-provoking talks. For me, science is a journey, and sharing it with others makes it even more exciting.

One of my big goals? To have my own observatory, away from the city lights and noises, where I can explore the universe with complete focus. The question of whether we are truly alone in the cosmos is one that fuels my curiosity—and I'm determined to find answers.

I even express science through music, writing songs that reflect my wonder about it.

In short, Science isn't just in my head; it's part of everything I do. I question it, share it, and build it. Science isn't just knowledge to me; it's a way of seeing and experiencing the world. And special thanks to Ecrintrn for helping me with design of my website and giving me ideas about coding.
